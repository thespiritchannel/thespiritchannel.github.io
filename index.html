These are the archives of the conference calls/internet broadcasts under the auspices of Intelligent-Infinity and Wynn Free, from the period from 2009 to the present. Wynn has become an interface between a group soul in a higher dimension who identifies itself as the Council of Elohim and the Earth realm. The Elohim take credit for the creation of the physical universe and have many wise and helpful observations regarding our realm. Many people have had permanent positive shifts as a result of listening to these calls.

*"I get a high (good vibes) when reading your material, attending your conference calls and listening to the audios."
*"On my First conference call Sun, I felt energy moving and my heart chakra open wide!"

There are 3 calls a week and each one has a particular emphasis:

 Sunday - Personal and Planetary Healing: This has been our most popular call. It focuses on bringing in the energy of the higher realms which many people on the line say they can feel.

Monday - Wildcard A variety of topics. Sometimes there is a channeling session with Wynn and the Elohim through his partner Terry Brown. A very popular ongoing series is How the Elohim Created the Universe Series

Wednesday - You Got Questions Listeners submit questions on a variety of topics, from deep personal challenges to various esoteric queries. The answers to the questions are channelled by Terry.

There are over 1200 sessions in the archives below. Have fun. If you'd like to listen to a live call, you can access the schedule at www.conference.intelligent-infinity.com. You can also go to www.intelligent-infinity.com to learn more and download Wynn's book - The Creator Gods of the Physical Universe want to talk to you.
