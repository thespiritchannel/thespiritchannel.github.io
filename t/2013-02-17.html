Your Preoccupying Thoughts Are Clues
To What You’re Working Through 

Sunday Morning Grid Healing  2.17.13

Host: Wynn Free
Channeled by Terry Brown
Transcribed by Cindy Izzo
Edited by Terry Brown, Valerie Hawes and Cindy Izzo
Formatted and sent by Gary Brownlee

 

Wynn calls in the Light:

 

Father-Mother God, we ask for the presence of the Light, to surround and protect Terry, myself and everyone on this line and everyone on the replays and any negativity be taken to the highest realms of Light and transmuted for the highest good of all concerned. We see ourselves in the flow of energy radiating from the center of the Universe, through the galaxies, through the Milky Way, through the outer energy fields of our solar system, through our solar system, through the energy fields of planet earth, through our bodies and into the center of the Earth. Right now, we invoke a group energy connection while maintaining the sovereign integrity of our souls and we invite those Sources that are positive, service-to-others, honoring The Law of One, to join with us. We create a protected space that only the positive has access to and anything not of that nature must leave now. We invite our Sources to speak to us Terry Brown.

 

Ra’An: We greet you in the Love-Light of The One Infinite Creator. We are honored to be with you this day, the 17th of February, 2013. We take a moment; we appear non-linearly in connection and in the vicinity of each and every person who wishes to be connected with and we are there for you. We honor free will, so that if someone doesn’t wish to connect or is shy or wants to check it out further, we do not connect unless you ask.

 

We are delighted and honored to be present here. This is Ra’An; we are of a number of different entities from the Ra group and the Elohim group. We are here in service to each and every person who wishes to connect. We are aware of what you have asked and we see that each and every one of you who have asked are asking for we are there for each and every one of you and we see the loving and the kindness that you have, those on the call and listening to the replays—and we see that you are reaching to help others as when you have put things in the Light. We see that many of you have put those that you love and care about in the Light for healing.

 

This is very helpful, as it is very supportive of these individuals that you have put into the Light. It brings in synchronicities; it brings in energies; it brings in frequencies of healing and it helps those individuals that wish to take advantage of that to move toward healing more easily. You are in service; you are doing a very important job of helping move the tone of those around you and the tone of those who are struggling to overcome their karma and struggling to heal and survive in the 3rd density.

 

You are moving to help them to be stronger to be able to move into their own purpose, their own path. We take a moment and we surround those whom you have put into the Light and yourselves in healing energies; in light energies that strengthen and that make the light body stronger and more able to connect and to come out of their shell and to strengthen them to move into their life path.

 

We look at Gracie and we think of and send this energy to Gracie. We look at Tammy and we give this energy, this supportive energy, this magnificiation of strength to her and to those that she has put into the light. To Joe, we see the connection strengthening and his purpose, his feet solidly on his path and the Light flooding in to his connections.

 

You all need connections, as it is a mutually dependent realm. You need your bodies to be able to connect and to function. You need your relatives; you need your mother and father, or those that raised you, either now or in the past. These individuals have helped with your growth. Often an individual has developed issues during their connection with their parents and relatives that they all needed to work through.

 

Working through the issues, things in your mind that you think about; these are areas that are clues to what you are working through. Clues to your path, clues to your purpose; for when you are in the 3rd density, it is the perfect opportunity to work through the issues that have set you on the wheel of karma. The things that you are seeking to learn, these are the things that are in your mind; these are the things that you are struggling with and these are the clues.

 

Where your thoughts are, are the clues of what you are working through. As you observe the things that you are working through and the injustices that you perceive, it may be some injustice appearing from a past life and an injustice that you are seeking to right and you do not wish to let go of it; although if you “just let it go” and walk out of it, then you would not need to recreate it.

 

But there is an issue or some injustice that you wanted to get across and you would like to have it righted before you let it go. You wish to have this resolved; at the base of karmic issues you have things that you have been trying to work through and you wish to be heard. When you wish to have something from the past resolved, very often the individual brings it to the present and in doing so, it energetically hooks up situations that bring the issue into the present and gives you another chance for the issue to resolve.

 

In what we have said here and in your thoughts that preoccupy your mind and that you seek to resolve, these are the areas where you need to work them through. One of the major things that help you is to forgive the other person. Forgive the other person even though there is an injustice.

 

This does not mean to go along with the other person. This does not mean to accept the other person’s point of view because you passionately do not. It means to see the situation as it is and allow it to be like it is for a moment, since it is that way anyway. Allow it and then give it the space to be able to resolve from that point and change.

 

Picture it changing; get a little book and write your grievance in the book. Write everything you can think of about your grievance and then take a little space and for the moment, see it as it is. It is like a hurricane coming through that you do not wish to have come through and it is like the hurricane that has ruined the house but it is like a fact of life. Then even though it is hard, see it the way that it actually is and then picture it beginning to resolve. Picture the house being rebuilt, the land healing, the space healing, the desperate feelings healing from however it was and moving into a total healing and a beginning of a total harmony.

 

Write in your little book about how perhaps the other person could see your point of view. Write out your own point of view about your mother not seeing something or your mother not understanding something and just write out your point of view to your mother and then allow all of the factors involved energetically to resolve in your own mind for if it resolves in your own mind it will begin to resolve in the other people. For all are one, all are love at the base and the bottom of everything.

 

Thank you.
