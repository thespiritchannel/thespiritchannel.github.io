Earth is a School; You Can Learn to Accept Your Good

 Wynn: calls in the Light:

Father- Mother God, we ask for the Presence of the Light, to surround and protect each person on this line and any negativity be taken to the highest realms of Light and transmuted for the highest good of all concerned. We see ourselves in the flow of energy radiating from the center of the Universe, through the galaxies, through the Milky Way, through the Solar System, through the outer energy fields of Planet Earth, through our bodies and into the center of the Earth. Right now, we invoke a group energy connection while maintaining the sovereign integrity of our souls. We invite those Sources that are positive, service to others, honoring The Law of One, to join with us and we create a protected space that only the Positive has access to and anything not of that nature must leave now.

We wait for this morning’s message through Terry.

 

Ra’An: We greet you in the Love Light of The One Infinite Creator. We are here with you. We have manifested in the atmosphere here of planet Earth and we are delighted to be available to each and every one of you. We do not come over the telephone lines; we do not come over the radio. This is the voice that comes over that. However, we come directly, nonlinearly into the space of everyone who asks. We are of free will, so we do not come into your space unless you have asked.

 

We see each and every one of you as Lights, energy Lights of a color that is of the color white and of a radiating light. 

 

We take a moment and we look at the things that you have put into the Light. We see that a number of you have put others that you love and care about into the Light. This is a help and a support, as we can then add our energies to these situations, to the people, to add our love, add our support. The individuals then have as a choice to take advantage or to not take advantage of this, as we do not invade their free will either.

 

We take a further moment to look at each and every one of your requests. We are non-linear and so we do not do things one at a time. We can do a multitude of things at one time and there are many of us. We are, in some of our areas of expertise, good at providing frequencies which are healing for those who request that.

 

At times, individuals are working through growth lessons and what is in front of them is the next lesson that they can benefit by in the Earth plane. Sometimes they are balancing karma and it may not be apparent that what is happening is the balancing of karma from something in the past that they have created, something that they have done, something they have not done. Then, at this time, the balancing of that is coming in. If they had done something of harm to another, then the balancing can come in and they could have a similar harm to them.

 

Sometimes it is asked, “Why did God bring this on me?” However if they were to look into their own self and into their own motivations, they would see an impetus of creation, a connection with the frequencies of that, that have come in. They have opened themselves to bring this in, to balance some karma from the past. This does not mean that the individual is a bad person, to have this happen. This is the Universal law, since all is One.

 

We take a moment. We have the ability, if an individual wishes, to simply lift that karma from them, to dissolve it and to open the door to letting in the Light of healing; the Light that can dissolve disruption, dissolve disharmony and in the opening of the door and the shedding of the Light, allow a person to choose to let that karma go and to pick up the life that brings you refreshing flows, refreshing love, refreshing abundance, to fill in the cracks that need has created and to bring in manifestation of what is needed for you to grow healthy and strong in that area.

 

Earth is a school; a place to grow, a place to move through situations and to learn about yourself, to learn to open yourself to accept the love and appreciation and gratitude that are there for you with a balanced karma. 

 

It is our gratitude to connect with each and every one of you that wishes to be connected with. It is a sacred moment in time and we love each and every one of you more than you can possibly imagine. We leave but we do not leave. We see an open door to step through for you who wish to step through. Thank you.
