We invite those Sources that are positive, service to others, honoring The Law of One, to join with us and we create a protective space that only the Positive has access to. Anything not of that nature must leave now.

Wynn:  Ok, we hear you, so go ahead.

Council of Ahn (through Daphne):  Greetings to you on this later day of February in your calendar year 2012. It has been some time since we have addressed this group and brought forth particulars of the experience of disseminating information regarding the Ascension process from our dimensionality into your dimensionality. It is a multi-tiered and multi-faceted process which moves in fits and starts and people will find themselves at different pathways and in different circumstances and at different levels of convergence with each other and with their connections to God-self and their Higher Self. Have you specific questions for us this day?

Wynn:  This morning I just wanted to have a greeting through Daphne for all the people here. Is there anything you’d like to share in terms of what a person can do personally in the best way to prepare themselves for the times we’re in?

Council of Ahn (through Daphne):  We thank you for your question and this is a very pertinent question. We are in times where the exterior influences, as you would say, the external media, the external frequencies of mass consciousness of the collective consciousness, are strong. As put a hypnotic affect, the hypnotic role in gathering people into a fearful or over excitable condition, whereby they would be reactive or overly reactive to external stimuli, which is not in fact the truth of the particular being’s situation, though the certain powers that be would have you think that it is the truth.

To remove yourselves from these influences is not, in today’s modern society, altogether easy. It is a process of discernment and selection and attunement with those forces which you find uplifting, with selective and limited exposure to media and this includes the Internet, to radio and television and to discern those Sources in your life which bring you a feeling of upliftment, a feeling of freedom, a feeling of joy and those sources which bring you either static or even worse a feeling of disruption, a feeling of entropy, a feeling of chaotic confusion.

 It is a process, an ongoing process, of weeding out and being able to literally attune oneself to that which is helpful and that which is non-helpful. Sometimes this means physically removing oneself from a situation. Sometimes when this is not possible, one has to come up with other creative solutions to work around the particular frequency which is causing the problem. That said, have you other questions.

Wynn:  Well, one thing that seems somewhat inevitable is an economic collapse. So, is it a good idea to store food at this time, and food and water to prepare for that, in case there are disruptions of transportation?

Council of Ahn (through Daphne):    Can you repeat the last sentence? Your voice is not clear on our end.

Wynn:  I was asking that many people are predicting an economic collapse and is this a good time to store some food and water in preparation for possible disruptions of transportation and sources.

Council of Ahn (through Daphne):    Yes, we understand your question, thank you for your question. In terms of probable reality at this point in the time-space continuum, as we see it, The Council of Ahn, it is still unknown. There are many factors at play.

However, it is always prudent to be as self-sufficient in your daily living habituation and your working habituation as you can be. Especially those who are living in the more rural areas who have access to a place they can either grow or store food, and have access also to perhaps a bartering system of some sort with neighbors and people in the local towns. This is to be encouraged, a self sufficiency of a more simple time, an earlier time.

You will find this comforting and very much more need-fulfilling than being caught up in the artificial commerce and the inflated structures of the false economy, which is in place now and running wild with people’s emotions, people’s fears, and people’s proclamations of who and what they are, and what ought to be done.

This entity now finds herself living in a part of the country which is not as flexible in its ideas in thinking as the western most part of the United States and Alaska where she most recently found herself for a number of years. So there’s an adjustment being made here which is not wholly easy or wholly smooth. And we can go into more of that later also for others of yourselves who find their physical environment somewhat at odds with their interior alignments. More questions?

Wynn:  No, I think that will be all and I thank you so much for coming forth and giving us this transmission through Daphne. And I welcome your presence through Daphne on the rest of our call.

Council of Ahn (through Daphne):    We are grateful to have the opportunity to speak with you. This is the Council of Ahn and we bid you good day. Adonai, Adonai, Pax Christos, Aum, Shanti, Shanti, Shantihi.

Wynn:  Thank you very much. And for those of you who are wondering, the Council of Ahn was the first intelligence that spoke to us through Daphne that was part of the Elohim. When I asked about who the Council of Ahn was, they said that it was the division of the Elohim that was responsible for converting a local language two ways into a form that the Elohim could understand.

And in terms of food, I would suggest that one of the best ways to do this is: if you can find a ninety-nine cents store or a Big Lots store or maybe there’s some store like that near you. They have lots of canned foods for a dollar, sometimes less, sometimes two for a dollar. When you are storing things, it’s best not to store acidic type foods, because the acid can tend to leach toxins from the metal of the can. But a hundred dollars can go really far in having a little extra reserve, like an insurance policy, just in case it’s ever needed.
