Wynn:  Father-Mother God, we ask for the Presence of the Light, to surround and protect each person here and any negativity be taken to the highest realms of Light and transmuted for the highest good of all concerned. We see ourselves in the flow of energy radiating from the center of the Universe, through the galaxies, through the Milky Way, through the solar system, through the outer energy fields of planet earth, through our bodies and into the center of the earth. Right now, we invoke a group energy connection while maintaining the sovereign integrity of our souls and we invite those Sources that are positive, service to others, honoring The Law of One, to join with us and we create a protected space that only the positive has access to and anything not of that nature must leave now.

We turn this over to our sources to bring us a special message through Terry this Sunday morning.

 

Ra’An:  We greet you in the Love Light of The One Infinite Creator. This is Ra’An. We are a combination group that has gotten together from the Elohim and from the Ra to be able to specifically work with your group and each and every individual on the line and each and every individual who wishes assistance.

 

We are able to give Love Light of a very high frequency. We, the Elohim, have joined with the Ra group because it makes a wider path, a wider frequency range, that individuals may be able to sense. We are there for each and every one of you in your space nonlinearly.  

 

We do not come over the telephone line. We do not come with Terry’s voice. We are nonlinear in your space, for each and every person who wishes to sense us, to make contact with us. In the group that we have on Sundays, in the bringing through of the energies from our group to your group with everyone on the line, it makes it easier for you to be able to focus and to sense our presence.

 

Sometimes some people may be able to sense a pink color that we have when we manifest into your realm; a pink color which surrounds you and which can be even visible as it was on one person’s computer, when the bar on his computer screen turned pink and he took a picture of it.

 

We are delighted to be with you. We are delighted to have this opportunity, this sacred opportunity, to connect with you.

 

The third density realm has been like an isolation ward, where people move into the frequencies of the third density and they do not focus upon the higher frequencies and so  they forget that they are part of One and All. 

 

You are part of One and All, and you are loved more than you can possibly imagine. You are loved by us. You are loved by The All That Is. You are loved by God. You are one with your brothers and sisters within the earth realm and beyond the earth realm.  

 

When you realize this, then the individual things that you have put into the Light can be put into perspective and you can see that, overall, you are fine. You are a part of a loving Universe. You are part and parcel [of it]. You are part of humanity. You are part of the higher realms and you are one, and each part is important and the whole does not come together right if one part would be missing. You are part of All.

 

So when you look at these things that you have put in the Light knowing that this is a temporary situation, it is not the permanent situation and based upon that, hopefully that can give you leverage to be able to put into perspective the feelings that you are working with right now, which are areas [in] which you are seeking growth, seeking learning, how to handle, how to become stronger in spite of these things, these barriers, these things that you are facing.

 

We yearn for you to find your way through these things and find that you can be the master of them and we are there to provide frequency assistance, loving assistance support. Sometimes moving out of the stuckness of your individual situation, shifting just a little bit, can bring you into the solution for whatever is stuck there for you, you need to shift in order to move into the healing solution, for if you just butt heads with the stuck situation as it is right now without making the shift, then you will find that you continue with the difficult situation.

 

There needs to be a shift. First of all, if you can grasp in your own mind and heart what the shift would look like, what it would feel like, if you can really sense it, then you have made the first step toward achieving that shift.

 

We send you joy. We send you love. We are here with you and we love each and every one of you. We leave but we do not leave. Thank you.
