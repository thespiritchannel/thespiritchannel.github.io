Ra’An:  We greet you in the Love Light of The One Infinite Creator. We are delighted to speak with you this Sunday, December 9, 2012.

 

We look through and at the earth plane and we make contact with the consensus reality and the earth plane and as so; we see the various times of the year where the individuals experience the joy of giving, the joy of connecting.  We see the joy of giving among the people and it is a beautiful time of year as this energy carries with it the beautiful vibrations of love and connection and desire to give and to be with each other.  

 

We connect with each and every person on the line who wishes to be connected with. We honor free will, so we only connect with each person who wishes that connection. We are taking a moment for this connection.

 

We do not connect through the telephone lines and we do not connect through the computer lines. The vibrations, the electrical grid, we do not connect through that, although we can extend our energies through that. When our energies add to that, it makes a channel of certain frequency that does not connect and it isolates our frequency, so we do not connect through those lines. We just connect directly with each and every one of you.

 

We take a look at the world situation. We see Japan in the Love Light. We see the various countries in the Love Light and we see the difficulties of group that different groups are having.

 

This is Ra’An; we are a collective from the Elohim and from the Ra group and we have joined to increase our frequency. We were chuckling when Wynn was talking of us being invisible because, to us, we are very physical. We are at a very high frequency. It is a frequency that is not commonly sensed within your spectrum of visibility and availability of perception.

 

We take a moment and we see Sandy in the light; the one who passed over last night and we see that she is between worlds still checking in periodically on her body and experiencing, on the other hand, the Love and Light of the higher realms. It takes a time sometimes after individuals have passed to set their transition and she is doing very well and she is there.

 

She does not know that she has passed in a real sense; however, there is a part of her that knows and is being contacted by the higher realms, so that when she is ready, she may receive the guidance she needs to move into the higher realms and into the heavenly realm. We take a moment to send her steadiness and support.

 

We see other things that people have put into the Light and we see that you are, as a group, listening and are learning about centering within your own self, so that the strength that you are, the Source that you are, can help you to be and connect with guidance.

 

We again look at the season, the season of winter in the Northern Hemisphere and as you move into the season of winter, the holidays bring people together and the spirit of togetherness wafts through the space and enlivens things and connects people and if you allow yourself, you can feel the love. You can feel the love simply in that consensus reality, in the music; for a moment, put the problems to the side, the sickness, and the pains; know that you are not the sickness; you are not the pains. You are much beyond that and it is the body instrument that is carrying those and as such, we send vibration of healing and alleviation of pain.

 

We thank you for this sacred moment to be together and we wish you a joyous season.

 

Thank you.
