We are really honored to have Daphne on the line tonight; she’s going to help us answer the questions. I’m sure most of you know who Daphne is, but if anyone is listening for the first time, Daphne is the inadvertent founder of our group. There was this question that I asked, “Does anybody want to talk to me?” and she answered. That’s what started everything on this fateful trip from Los Angeles to Portland, Oregon.

 

One of the things that many of you are aware of in the history, if you’ve read our materials, is this idea of negative attacks. I didn’t understand negative attacks; I didn’t understand any of this. I had a chance to observe Daphne for quite a bit of time, trying to figure out why she was having this experience and how it worked and asking questions about it. It’s something, particularly when Daphne is on the line, that we have to be careful about.

 

I have absolute certainty there is such a thing as the negative in other dimensions. They have their agendas and this kind of work in particular that we’re doing is not in their plans; it’s disruptive. When we’re asking questions to our Sources and we ask about the negative, it has the potential—even by me talking about them right now; I’m just going to say this and then we’re going to clear the space—it has the potential to get their attention. They’re kind of psychic. It’s walking a fine line to ask about them especially in public on these calls. Some of you who have been a regular attendee and listening know, that there’s at least one time where we really put our foot in the mud. The energy of the call got really tacky and there was a whole explanation given by the Sources.

 

I know that there’s a lot of curiosity about that level. It’s understandable to have curiosity about it. I know a lot of the answers myself. Terry doesn’t seem to fall into quite the quagmire that Daphne has the potential to do. I’ve asked a question in a session with Terry, I don’t think it was a public session, about this particular topic. They said there was a certain amount that was important for people to understand so that they’d have more discernment.

 

The problem with what we’re calling the negative is that it’s tricky; it can misrepresent itself. It can tell you they’re Jesus; it can tell you they’re the Elohim; they can channel. When you’re trying to look for things doing Google searches, you very easily end up in a location that you may not want to end up in and you might have been tricked.

 

I certainly have been tricked in other lifetimes; I’m sure of it. I know particular instances where I thought something was Positive and it wasn’t and I became supportive of it. It goes with the territory. There is probably enough information in our archives. I know you’re reading them; you’ll find some of this out; there’s also information in the Ra Material about it.

 

Of course you know when Carla Rueckert did the Ra Material, how much she was negatively attacked; almost died. She got to understand that it was that important to get this stuff out into the world that she was willing to risk that and go through a lot of suffering to do it. Of course, she and I are working together.

 

I think part of the reason that we have such good affinity is that we’ve both come to a place where we’ve had not the same experiences, but complementary experiences, in terms of what the negative is about and how they work and how they attempt to screw things up down here.

 

Being that I said all that, I’ll say a couple of things here, just as general overview comments:

 

The Positive does not create fear. So, for example, if you historically go into the Bible and there’s a God that’s the God of fear, the God that’s supposed to make you tremble, the God that tells you to sacrifice your children, then you can rest assured that it’s not a positive Source, even when everyone uses that name and thinks it’s God; it’s not the highest of highs.

 

The Positive does not take your decision-making power away; it supports it. It supports your discernment; it never encourages you to give up your power. It never tells you to follow it. It never says, “I am the only way”.

 

Another thing that I’ve observed: I’ve never asked this question, but I think this is a fairly good observation. Anything that tells you that deliverance is in the future has got something mixed in its polarity. For example, ‘things are screwed up now but when spaceships land things are going to be okay; they’re going to save us’, is very likely of the negative. If somebody says a certain person is going to return here and when he returns everything is going to be fine, chances are, that’s not positive or it’s mixed.

 

If you notice, on our calls, many of you notice this, there’s an energy present which is totally positive, totally uplifting and comes into your system. You can feel depressions leave, you can feel things change and it’s present, right now. You see, there is no future; there is only the now. In the now we carry our memories and our past life track. It’s all part of the giant illusion which we call life. We’re in the illusion.

 

The goal is: How do we raise our position in the illusion so we’re above things rather than buried by them? The Sources we’re dealing with have the ability to come down from way high in the illusion and connect with us so we feel it. From my experience and everything I’ve heard from people, the feeling is never taking you over; it’s never displacing you. It’s like part of you. In the Law of One there’s no separation; what’s above is part of what’s below.

 

It’s like in that song, “Look out for Mr. In-Between” because that’s where the negative exists. Do you know this song? “Look out for Mr. In-Between…” I always wondered why I liked that song; it was an old George Gershwin song. When you’re coming from a low place, which could be called a human place, then you’re accessing the energy of a high place, some of the intermediary places are kind of not energetically complete. There’s not a complete flow from the low to the high. If there was, there wouldn’t be a problem because there would be no negative getting through. It’s in the intermediary places that there are chinks in the armor where negative energies, negative entities in other dimensions, can have access.

 

That’s why this kind of work is not to be messed with casually; because if you want to access the highest realms, you have to be prepared to clear or cope with the middle realms where negative attacks can occur.

 

Some people, some of you, even, get stuck in that area and it gets hard to get out of, because you’ve let something through. You have to be careful; see if you can understand why I am so cautious on these calls and why I say to be so careful about channeling.

 

Channeling is exactly the way the negative gains its influence in this realm. When a voice is talking to you through someone else, how do you know it’s not negative? How do you know you’re not being misled? How do you know they’re not trying to turn you into their patsy? That’s why you’ve got to be really careful; that’s why it’s really good not to start making a living doing this.

 

If you start making a living being a perpetrator of channeled information you now get very attached, you can’t help it. We’re all attached to making money and making a living. By making a living on channeled information, there’s a huge potential for the negative to come in and take it over, because now you’re dependent on your living. If you start being a voice for something negative, you can’t stop. You can’t stop because you won’t pay your rent.

 

It’s one reason that I can stop this at anytime. It’s one of the reasons our little pendant business helps to support this. It’s not depending on what we do as an output to make money. That means that I don’t have to worry about paying the bills and if a negative source comes through I better let them talk. That doesn’t mean a negative source won’t ever come through on these calls; it’s possible. One has to always keep your guard up, keep your discernment up; always learn to feel the energies of the positive. As you feel those energies, it’s kind of a second validation that you’ve got the right Sources.

 

Carla Rueckert or, it might have been Don Elkins, her channeling partner, made the comment when she started on the Ra Material, that the way to protect yourself from the negative is to never ask a question that’s not an eternal truth. In other words, the same answer would hold 10,000 years from now as now, which means it’s a truth of life. When you ask questions that are of the moment of time, you very much open yourself up to the possibility of a negative source.

 

Daphne and Terry are both in the middle of this; I’ll maybe let them make a comment on this; it’s an area that if I didn’t live through it I could never understand it. It’s not anything I could understand by reading about it or thinking about it. In other lifetimes, I know I was fooled and tricked. When you’re going around and looking on the Internet and doing searches: ‘2012’, ‘DNA evolution’, you will most definitely find, in my opinion, people putting out messages that are negative.

 

If a source says they’re Jesus, you’d better read it really carefully before you think it’s really Jesus. In many cases, using the name of somebody that’s famous and respected, like Jesus, is a temptation to ask people to give up their power and look for anything like that in terms of giving up your power.

 

If you happen to be a Christian, see if you feel the same resonance as a resonance to Jesus’ words in the New Testament; see if you feel a resonance with that or not, okay?; because it is tricky, tricky, tricky, tricky. If you’ve ever been duped by a good salesperson, you know how easy it is for someone to look good, get into your space and then lead you down the wrong direction.

Am I putting you on the spot?
