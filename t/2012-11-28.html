Wynn:  It is Wednesday, November 28, 2012. This is one of our three calls that we do every week; each call is different. On our Wednesday call, people submit questions to me.

 

My name is Wynn Free; I’m the guy that asks questions. If you’ve read the book, this is somewhat of a continuation of the book. If you’re someone listening who has not listened to all of our calls, the ones during the week are different; they have different approaches.

 

The Monday call is kind of a seat-of-the-pants call; we do different things. Sometimes I will pick a topic and we will question our Sources through Terry and sometimes Daphne. Often Carla Rueckert is my guest; in the world of people who channel, Carla is a world-class person, who influenced me and influenced David Wilcock with her Ra channelings. They’re very deep and oftentimes we will discuss some aspect of what came through her.

 

Our Sunday call, which has grown to be our most popular call, is a little hard to describe in words. I’ll make an attempt: it’s a call where many of us actually experience what group energy is. We’re actually creating a group energy. Don’t ask me how we do it. It was an experimental call for quite some time. I got so many emails from people who could feel the energy on the line. If you want to visualize it, we’ve been communicating for a long time with these Sources that identify themselves as group souls.

 

One group says they created the physical Universe and one of the group souls is composed of graduates of the physical Universe that got off the wheel of reincarnation. Because of the nature of how this unfolded and because we were making connections with both of them individually, they both made some kind of pact to work together with us.

 

I don’t understand how this works and neither can you; I’m just telling you what they say and it gives us a wide-spectrum of experience. They say they can move into our realm in a manner so we can experience them. When I said that, some of you might have felt that there was a sudden shift of energy. They say they’re watching us; they say they can tune into us individually and that they can hear our thoughts. It’s my take that many times in history, these two group souls have been one of the Sources that answer the call for God.

 

They’re not God in the traditional way, but probably a better name for God is All-That-Is and they’re a bigger aspect of All-That-Is than anything else. The One Infinite Creator is all that is; if it separated itself from being All That Is, it would be some ‘thing’ and that would be an individuation.

 

This has to do with time. As much as it looks like a fixed reality to most of us in this realm, time is not a fixed reality; you can move in and out of it. You can move into other timelines. The Universe—let’s not say the Universe, let’s look at our Universe. We are living on a 3D timeline, the timeline that is in this dimension. Even though we are living in it, we are expanding all the time into other timelines.

 

At the top of all of this, time stops; or there’s no time. Just on the edges of where time stops is where we might find this group soul that is the Elohim. They can move through all the timelines; they don’t move their bodies. They don’t have bodies; at least that’s the way they describe themselves.

 

They’re pure energy; they can move through all the timelines and they can be in your bedroom or your living room. You’re not going to see them, but you can feel them, many people can feel them. As you come onto these calls, you seem to learn how to tune in to their frequency. 

 

We’re going to be asking questions in just a moment to these group souls. We’re going to find out their answers. One of the things about them is that they don’t take away your free will; they don’t tell you what to do; they tell you how to figure out what to do; they give you wise looks at things that you may not have thought of. The purpose of all that we do is that even though there’s a lot of information and a lot of wisdom that comes through these calls, there’s something else that happens and that is this ‘energy transmission’. Part of the verbal part of the call is to help distract or engage your mind, because usually most people are caught in their minds and they’re thinking about things. They think, “What should I do? How should I do it?”

 

This whole experience comes from being a little bit off-center; not directly approaching it. All the time during these calls there are energies that are in your periphery that some of you are going to feel. When you feel them you’re going to say, “Could that be it? Is that what Wynn is talking about?”

 

You’re going to have to make up your own mind. This is not scientific, in the sense that we’re trying to prove anything or claim anything; it’s just the product of me having ten years of conversations with them and learning how they work and how they interface with us as well as creating opportunities for other people to share in that. That is what we’re doing right now.

 

On our Wednesday call, we have questions that Terry, who is able to be a voice for these Sources, is going to answer right here on the line. I thank every one of you for being here. If you’ve never attended our Sunday call I hope you’ll try it out, because that’s where we focus on feeling the energies and so many people do.

 

Are you ready, Terry?

 

Terry:  Yes. Now I’m ready; as ready as I’ll ever be, I guess.

 

Wynn:  That’s right; we’re the Never-Ready Team who shows up.

 

Terry:  Yes, we just show up and see what happens.

 

Wynn:  We show up and see what happens. You can all show up and share in this adventure of what’s going to happen.

 

We have this thing of calling in the Light; it’s an invocation to help get the energies more focused and more high, to create what Carla Rueckert calls “the tuning”, to bring in the space so our Sources can communicate with us.

 

Father-Mother God, we ask for the presence of the Light, to surround and protect Terry and myself and everyone on this line and any negativity be taken to the highest realms of Light and transmuted for the highest good of all concerned. We see ourselves in the flow of energy radiating from the center of the Universe, through the galaxies, through the Milky Way, through the Solar System and through the outer energy fields of planet Earth. Right now, we invoke a group energy connection while maintaining the sovereign integrity of our souls. We invite those Sources that are positive, service-to-others, honoring The Law of One, to join with us as we create a protected space that only the Positive has access to. Anything not of that nature must leave now.

 

We’ll let our Sources greet us and we’ll go through the questions for this evening.

 

Ra’An:  We greet you in the Love Light of The One Infinite Creator. We are here in the Universe and outside of the Universe. We are non-linearly available to each and every one of you. We are at your beck and call and we respond to the desires and the intentions for individuals to connect with us. We bring with that our energies and our desires and our intention for the highest good of all of you.

 

Do you have questions?

 

Wynn:  Yes. This is a question from Chris. His question is:

 

Does casting magic spells to improve your own life go against God’s will? Are there repercussions in doing this? What is the general significance of using what’s called “magic”?

 

I would like to say: I think the word “magic” is a very broad word that has a lot of different definitions. I think that Chris is using the word in the sense of being able to apply your willpower in such a way as to make something happen through your willpower. Perhaps it’s got some kind of connection to prayer, because there’s an intent to cause something to occur. So it’s an interesting question; oftentimes magic is used without the proviso of ‘for the highest good of all concerned, or honoring free will’, in which case, it would probably be of a different nature.

 

Let’s let our Sources answer that question for tonight. Thank you.

 

Ra’An:  When one gives a question we will be able to focus better if they give their location where they are, so that we can connect very clearly with the person with that question.

 

We can begin to answer.

 

We see that there are ways that the Universe has been put together to work like clockwork and with harmony, the music of the spheres, the vibrations working in harmony with each other. There is harmony and there is disharmony.

 

Some individuals tend to use magic to force something; to bring something into their sphere that would not have ordinarily been there. In such a case, they can be overpowering other beings with their intention, and numbing them and subjugating them so that they fall under the power, the hypnotic power, of another. We say that this; if a person does this, it has its own consequences in that, at some point or other, the individual can come more easily under the spell of another.

 

As they have opened up the gate to that frequency, they can be open to having the intentions of others take them over also, at similar frequencies. Then they themselves, having numbed another’s awareness or intentions and taken over their space and their path forward, then they themselves are open for the opposite reaction in the similar vibration to take them over.

 

Magic, when done in a magic show, is very entertaining and there is the expansiveness that a person can experience and the looking at it can begin to expand their horizons and open up the door to their creativity and expansiveness in the ability to bring in other ideas that they may not have thought of. There is a magic in the way things are put together and an orientation that a person needs to fall into to be able to hold a space or a location and a focus within a certain place and time.

 

Those are some of our comments.

 

Wynn:  Is there karma from doing magic; in other words, if one is using intentions to create results? What are the circumstances where karma is created?

 

Ra’An:  Karma is created, as we were mentioning, as one is doing it to another so they then, being at that level of operation, are more open to having it done to them. They set into a pattern, something within their own life that may unexpectedly influence them in ways that they did not know.

 

Wynn:  In other words, if you do that to other people you open the door for someone to do that to you reciprocally?

 

Ra’An:  That is correct. That is correct, which would have its own karma.

 

Wynn:  Yes, okay. Thank you. The question Michael asks is:

 

“Where am I going when I’m not conscious in-between your opening prayers, healing prayers and Mother Earth’s?”

 

He spaces out and loses track of things, particularly on our Sunday calls. He asks,

“Where is he going?”

 

Ra’An:  Thank you.

 

Your body is still in its space and time, where you are located. Your soul, your being, is working at higher frequencies, spaces and time and you are moving into vestiges of a higher timeline. You might notice at some point and some people might notice, that time tends to go faster when you are in that portion of the call.

 

Wynn:  Thank you; is that the answer?

 

Ra’An:  Yes.

 

Wynn:  I would assume that means Michael is moving into other dimensions, yes?

 

Ra’An:  Yes and other dimensions are in the same—we would say they are taking up the same area, but moving into, we don’t wish to use the word ‘parallel reality’, but it is in a sense, a parallel reality. By ‘parallel’ it is ever and always the same but at a different frequency; the location is guided by the frequency where one is being.

 

Wynn:  Thank you.

 

You know, just while we’re talking about that idea: is it possible that someone has two different bodies, one in this realm and one in another dimension? They can’t really see through that body, but yet the same energy stream is activating both bodies?

 

Ra’An:  Yes, you have more than one body; in that you have your physical body; you have your astral body; you have your mental body and they are all tracking and focused together. When you move to a different timeline you are merely changing the focus.

 

Wynn:  Yes. Okay. This is a general question:

 

Obviously there are a lot of changes going on in our planet right now. According to our understandings, there are changes going on in the energy fields all around us. We’re at the end of the 75,000 year cycle. The question is:

 

“What should our focus be in the next few months?”

 

Ra’An:  We don’t like ‘should’ that much; we see that you would do best if your focus was on remaining centered within your own self, as when you are centered and when you are the Source of your energy, when you are the one who is in charge of you, you then can deal better with whatever comes your way. You can be the Source.

 

If something suddenly changes, you can change also with it, because you are not depending upon your location to be centered there, or on your money to be centered there. You’re centered within your own self as your own Source and therefore you can roll with the punches. You can move with whatever is going on and you can be more resourceful.

 

Wynn:  Thank you. This is a question from Edna and Colleen. Colleen is in British Columbia and Edna is in Washington.

 

A few days ago, they were both talking on the phone and a banded rainbow appeared on the wall in her living room; this was Colleen’s living room. “The Sun was very low in the sky and it appeared directly in my view as we were talking; it only lasted about five minutes. It was so unusual that I mentioned it to Edna.”

 

“Was this appearance, at that moment, the sign from higher Sources or was it coincidence?” They were discussing something about a health issue and she was wondering if it related that they were making some indication about this health issue.

 

Ra’An:  In looking at this, we see that all is a part of it, so that each thing that was happening at that moment between the two of you was a part of all of the surrounding energy and the connection with the surrounding energy. We see that your own frequencies, your own looking, your own connections and the Sun connection were making a whole statement.

 

What we say is; all is a part of it. You, within your own self, have a knowing of the resonance that was taking on the space and connecting with the space at that moment in time.

 

We don’t wish to evaluate this for you, but for you to look and see how the conversation of the moment connected up with your own resonance of the looking at the health issue. We don’t wish you to read more into it than was there and you, within your own resonance, know what was valid and what should be gleaned from the conversation at that moment.

 

Wynn:  Thank you.

 

This is from Terric in Minneapolis. He had an incident during the spring of 2011 where he was sleeping. While he was asleep, around three or four a.m., he woke up and felt a presence on the right side of his bed. He opened his eyes and he saw what seemed to be a dark figure with dark cloths covered from head to toe. He was scared and he screamed his wife’s name. “When I tried to show her what was there, the figure disappeared.”

 

“Could you tell me what that was about and if it was something negative? If it was, what can I do to protect myself?”

 

Ra’An:  Thank you. Give us moment.

 

We suggest that you clear the space within the bedroom, perhaps smudging it with sage; that you, within your own self, ask for the space to be cleared and that you, within your own life, look at the things that are happening and clear up anything that may be not entirely straight-forward.

 

We do not see the presence as being harmful, but as being a sign that there may be something within your life that could bring disharmony or upset and to look at this time to clear this space, to clear up any disharmony, to set things right that may need to be right and to keep your health in good shape.

 

Wynn:  Thank you. Roseanne asks a general question about racism, stereo-typing.  Now with society’s ability..

 

Ra’An: Wait; you are breaking [up]; we are not understanding you.

 

Wynn:  Here we go; sorry. Lasanne asked a question. She’s curious about racism, stereo-typing and now that there’s the ability to reach larger spheres of people through social networking, it has given rise, in her eyes, to cyber-bullying:

 

“It seems we have a default for hatred of one another as humans”—she’s making assumptions there—“and I find it tiresome and frustrating personally, but is this a trait? Why do people find it so easy to denigrate one another without even getting to know each other first?” That’s from Lasanne.

 

Ra’An:  Thank you. One of the things that happens is that when a child grows up they are subject to the prejudices of their parents. In subtle ways, they pick this up and tend to carry on the beliefs of their parents. Even if, in other ways they rebel against the parents, there are subtle things that they pick up and they still believe as a basic.

 

There are other things that society carries forward. There are views that society has believed and has even put into practice. Although there are changes that have occurred and that individuals and minorities have gained more equality, still the prejudices from the past are lurking in the minds of individuals that have been raised by parents and grandparents and so forth back, that have come from that past. There are unlooked-at ideas that never get examined.

 

There is one other thing: that because of the prejudices, groups in power sometimes use those prejudices to exert control over other people. One can, within their own self, examine any prejudices that they have been taught and are just simply appearing there without examination; and upon examining their own responses, can begin to clear out any adverse responses of prejudice.

 

Wynn:  Thank you.

 

Is there any new information concerning December 21, 2012, which is something a lot of people have been anticipating?

 

In our last question about this it was said that it’s going to fairly well come and go; nothing particularly special was going to happen on that date. I know there are still a lot of people anxious, so I’d like to ask for a current update as of right now.

 

Ra’An:  Thank you. There are individuals who believe different things very strongly concerning December 21 and that there could be an end of time; some people there are, like Terrence McKenna, who do not see any future and others have not been able to see anything after December 21, going on for maybe a couple hundred years before they begin to see futures again.

 

However, when we look at the area, we are looking at a planet that is in transition. We are looking at a planet that is within the Solar System that is moving through the Milky Way galaxy and as such, it is subject, where it is located at this time, to an increasing energy bombarding the Solar System. There are books written upon that. 

 

In the past there have been changes in the crust of Earth, wherein there has been a slippage in the crust of Earth, catapulting the crust of the Earth, in relationship to the core, being a slippage. As such, you have mammoths that were grazing in a field and suddenly were transported into the cold arctic and flash-frozen. There are potentials for crustal slippage to happen at some time in the future; we do not see it happening for a while. We see you are questioning, “What is a while?” We do not have any exact answer for you.

 

We look further down to the basics of existence and we see that all is created in the now. As such, things being created can be changed, for at that particular time the changing of the foundations of people’s beliefs can move into a different outcome.

 

We see that although people do not like to look at this, their physical bodies have a time, after which they do transition. We look at what is important is being your own being, your own Source of energy in your connection with All That Is around, you so that you can move with whatever is happening. It is simply a new chapter that one moves into.

 

When one takes the position that they are a ‘soul-traveler’ from lifetime to lifetime, then they can position themselves in the center of their life. As such, they can begin to have the view that they are going to be okay no matter what in the physical happens; no matter if the GPS coordinates change. If they have their own center as their GPS center, then they can orient to whatever circumstances they find and can move into another beautiful existence.

 

Wynn:  Thank you.

 

There have been a number of small earthquakes on the West Coast recently; they’re not major; they’re small. I know that we’ve been asking for small earthquakes on our Sunday grid healing to release the stress of the Earth. Are the prayers of people helping to cause this?

Okay, thank you all for being here. Thanks to all our volunteers; thank you all who have been buying our products that we put out, because it really helps support our work and keep the work free.
