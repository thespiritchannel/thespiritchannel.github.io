Meditation is to still your mind, go into the center of yourself and feel yourself; who you really are. Of course, there are techniques: there are breathing techniques; there are all kinds of things that people will teach you: “If you do this, you will have a deeper meditation.”

 

My take on this is, that every one of us is in our own reality-box. Do you follow what I mean by that? We have a reality system. Some people have more expanded reality systems and some people have more contracted reality systems. Your reality system sets the limits of your ability to perceive the world around you, because you have to perceive it through your reality system.

 

When you start to meditate, usually your experience of the meditations will stay within your reality system no matter what you do, with minor variations. You’ll be meditating and you’ll be thinking of your children, or your wife, or naked women, or “I’ve got to eat” and “I’ve got to get my mind to stop”. Yet it all dances around your reality system. To have a really successful meditation, you have to expand beyond your reality system.

 

One of the things we’re doing on these calls is setting a reference point for a rather large reality system. Particularly on our Sunday call. If you’re not coming into our Sunday call and you’re curious about our work, you owe it to yourself to check it out.

 

On the Sunday call we focus on the experience of that expanded reality. On our Monday and Wednesday calls we’re talking about information and there is the expanded reality on the call, but it’s not focused upon. Our Sunday call is focused on it.

 

Once you have the experience of expanding beyond your own reality system, you can then have a reference point. When you go in to say, “I want to meditate” and you shut your eyes, immediately you have the reference point of that expanded space and you can potentially go there.

 

Even thinking about someone you love, if you really love somebody, when you think about them you move into an expanded reality. I would venture to speculate that if you really love someone, when you think of them your energy-field moves to where they are and if you imagine you can feel them inside of you, how it feels to feel them, then you’re probably there.

 

When I have this experience with somebody, I usually have little phenomena that go along with it. In their group energy they are combining energies and they go into an expanded space because of that.
