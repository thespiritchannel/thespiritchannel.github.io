Wynn calls in the Light:

 

Father-Mother God, we ask for the presence of the Light to surround and protect every one of us and any negativity be taken to the highest realms of Light and transmuted for the highest good of all concerned. We see ourselves in the flow of energy radiating from the center of the Universe, through the galaxies, through the Milky Way, through the solar system, through the outer energy fields of planet earth, through our bodies and into the center of the Earth. Right now, we invoke a group energy connection while maintaining the sovereign integrity of our souls. We invite those Sources that are positive, service-to-others, honoring The Law of One, to join with us. We create a protected space that only the positive has access to and anything not of that nature must leave now. 

 

Ra’An: We greet you in the Love Light of The One Infinite Creator. We are with you this day, planet Earth; we are not only with you but we are also at the same time in the higher realm. We are non-linear, so we are able to be at more than one place at a time, more than one density at a time; for all is one. We are in the Heavenly realm; we are higher than the Heavenly realm.

 

We are able to focus on more than one thing at a time; therefore we are available to each and every one of you. We do not come over the telephone line nor do we come through the radio; we come directly. We see your soul signatures; we see the soul signatures of the individuals who have put things in the Light and your wishes, your intentions, your true heart-felt intentions for healing for yourselves, as well as for your friends and for your family. We lend our intention, to add to your intention towards healing.

 

We ask you to check your belief systems, because your belief systems act as an intention. Perhaps your belief systems come from consensus reality or medical establishments, your government or community, genetics, family history or where you were raised, the beliefs of your parents, grandparents or great-grandparents; your beliefs have ended up being mixed in with your intentions for healing. 

 

Some of these may be in conflict; wherein, perhaps you have been given a medical diagnosis which is in conflict with what you truly desire in your heart.

 

When you become your own person, your own centered self, take a look at all these other influences and set your own frame, your own intention which your body then hears. Your body is alive. All of the tissues in your body are listening, all are responding.

 

So when you get mad at your body for not responding in the way that you wish, your body hears that and has consciousness and the area that’s bothering you has consciousness, so getting mad at your body makes it harder for that body part to wish to heal, because it does not feel understood. 

 

If you can be like we are, sharing your intention and all of your belief systems, and then dissolving the belief systems that you do not wish to respond to, your body will do better if those belief systems then become a strong support system for your body. How much love quotient does your body have? How much does your body feel loved? How much does the body part that’s bothering you feel that it is loved? 

 

If it could feel loved and supported then it could do better towards healing. Love is a healing vector, so make your body feel loved and cared for and nurtured; do something nice for your body; get a massage. Let your body know that it is loved; then it will do more to support you back. All is consciousness and all responds. So the connection is magnified through love; the support is magnified through love; through the sharing of love and the desire as you love your body or love your surroundings, then it gets it.

 

It has consciousness and it wishes to support you back. We hope this has made sense to you.

 

We love you; we support you non-linearly in your space where you are. We are of a very high frequency and we can carry the signature of the color pink and you may experience this light pink color when you tune in to our frequency. 

 

We are there supporting you; you do not have to sense us for us to be there supporting you, however we are there supporting you. We hear your intentions; we hear what you wish. This is a sacred moment in time, when we have the opportunity to connect with each and every one of you who wishes to be connected with. 

 

We love you more than you can imagine. Thank you for this opportunity to connect with those of you wishing connection. 

 

We leave but we do not leave.
