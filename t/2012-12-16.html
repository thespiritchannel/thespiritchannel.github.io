Wynn (Calls in the light)

 

Father-Mother God, we ask for the presence of the Light to surround and protect Terry, myself and every one of you on this line and any negativity be taken to the highest realms of Light and transmuted for the highest good of all concerned. We see ourselves in the flow of energy radiating from the center of the Universe, through the galaxies, through the Milky Way, through the energy fields of our solar system, through the outer energy fields of planet Earth, through our bodies and into the center of the Earth. Right now, we invoke a group energy connection of all of us present and we invite those Sources that are positive, service-to-others, honoring the Law of One to join with us. We create a protected space that only the positive has access to and anything not of that nature must leave now.  We’re going to turn this over to our sources as they speak to us through Terry.

 

 

Ra’An: We greet you in the Love Light of The One Infinite Creator. We are delighted to speak with you this day in December. We see that the energies are difficult at this time. They appear to be difficult because they are loosening up the locked down emotions that you and your companions travelling through the 3rd density have not been permitted to release. As these energies of Pluto and the configurations of the planets in relationship to the star systems are coming through, they are making it harder to keep these emotions in lockdown. 

 

As individuals keep themselves and their emotions locked up fiercely and attempt to maintain sociability, they are experiencing, we might say jostling, of these locked geometries of these emotions and some of them are bursting out. The resentments and extreme jealousies over how others are getting the love, abundance and things they so dearly want, are coming into view and conflict in such a way that they are bursting out. This is made more complex by the things that children have learned while growing up; some of them concerning video games and how they have learned to express their aggressions in the video games and then break out into the real world. This brings great sadness to individuals who are experiencing the effect of this. 

 

We see that extreme jealousy is at the bottom of it. Much of this is because individuals feel that they deserve the love, affection and abundance that others are getting and they get very frustrated in seeing that they do not have it. Going deeper, it goes to the point where they believe or have been taught that they are unworthy and as such, they lock themselves out and do not feel that they can even try to connect in the normal social ways because they have been taught that they are unworthy and are undeserving of this. 

 

This discrepancy in their own hearts creates much of the problem and distress so our message to you is to love your children and let them know it. Love yourself and know that you are worthy. Know that you can reverse any opinion you have of being unworthy; reverse it and know that you are worthy of love and abundance. Shift it around; you can switch yourself around so that you can get the love and abundance that you want; that it is there waiting for you when you work through situations of how to bring it to yourself by knowing within that you are worthy of that abundance and love. You can then begin to attract it to yourself without any blocks on it.

 

Thank you.
