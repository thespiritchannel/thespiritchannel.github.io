Then what happens is the person who is expanded is contracting and over a period of time they can easily forget themselves and lose themselves through the contraction.  Then, they reincarnate, I think, in that contracted energy and it becomes a loop that becomes hard to get out of.  To me, one way of dealing with that – you could say, “I’m leaving this person” – but, who is to say you’re going to find a person who is expansive.  If you want to find something expansive you have live an expansive life because you attract to you what you’re living.

That’s why you may have to contract to love them the way they need to be loved.  So you need to create another way to match your expanded matrix to keep it alive, otherwise it gets lost.  It can be in a spiritual group, it could be a church, it could be the meetings we do, it can be some kind of service – but, something so you don’t get stuck in that contraction.

 

If you try to explain this to the person you are with, they’re never going to understand it and they’re going to feel hurt, because it sounds to them like you’re being better than them just to preach it.  So you have operate on all of this, making your decisions unto yourself.
