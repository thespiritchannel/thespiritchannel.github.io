Gijs:  Dear Father-Mother God, One Infinite Creator, we ask for the presence of the Love Light. We ask for the Love Light to surround, protect and to connect with each person who is here with us, at this moment or later. We ask for any negativity to be taken to the highest realms of Light and for it to be transmuted and dissolved for the highest good of all concerned. We see ourselves in the flow of energy radiating from the center of the Universe, through all the galaxies, through our galaxy, through the Solar System and through all the planets coming into the outer energy fields of Earth, entering our bodies, flowing through us and radiating from our feet into the center of Mother Earth. Right now, we invoke a group energy connection amongst all of us present, while we maintain the sovereign and integrity of our souls. We ask for only Positive Sources, honoring The Law of One, free will and who operate for the highest good of all concerned. We ask these Sources to blend their energies with our energies and to create a protected space that only these Sources have access to and anything not of that nature must leave now.

We are all One and while being surrounded by the creative Christed Love Light, we await our Sources to speak to us through our dear friend, Terry Brown.

Ra’An:  We greet you in the Love Light of The One Infinite Creator. Today is February 5, 2012; Planet Earth, and we are in your sphere. We are connecting with each and every person on the line who wishes to be connected with and we send you Love and Light, greetings and health.

We send health to Gary and take a look at his particular issues and send Love Light and send the flow of energy to be corrected and to pick up in its tempo; to release any stuck points.

We similarly send Love Light to Marilyn, for circulation and for any stuck points to be released in her body. That the circulation may reach all points of her body and bring nutrients and health throughout her body.

We send support to Gina for the whales and the dolphins, and we send support for the issues with the landlord to become unstuck, and to start moving, and to reach a resolution. To the whales and the dolphins we make connection, as we are already connected with the whales and the dolphins and we reaffirm that with each and every individual who is working with us on the line that wishes to.

As we touch the whales and the dolphins, they send us their joy back at the connection, as they love the connection and the vibrancy of the interdimensional link-up that we always have, but which is widened and expanded on this call.

We send healing for Cinda and for William, for physical healing and mental healing and strength of the relationship.

We take a look at the Mideast and we see individual agendas leading to other individual agendas. We see individuals in reaction to trumped up messages that are placed there by people with agendas.

From there we look at the people in the Mideast; the people who are affected, the people who are struggling to put their families together, to make a good life for their families and we support their cause and their individual efforts. We speak slowly, as we are with those people supporting them, sending them strength.

We send and look at the situation with Ronnie, Don’s brother-in-law, and take a moment with this.

To the entangled souls of the Earth wherever they may be, we send support and we send Love Light and we send individual impulses to each of the souls, that they may find themselves and regain their dignity and individuality and as such they may lose all of the karmic distraughtness and slavishness and the frustrations, that they may become free and again know self-respect and dignity, and be again connected with their fellows, their fellow men, in a connection of harmony and give and take.

To Steve, we are looking at the connection between him and his father and send support for strengthening this relationship in a way that benefits both, for the good of both, as this is what they desire.

To Gijs’ niece and the three babies, we send support and joy, as there is much joy around this birth.

We thank Gina for putting the nuclear facilities in the Light; Fukushima and Nagasaki, any cities that have been affected by nuclear events. As we contact these areas, we note that particularly in Fukushima there is radiation, unhealthy radiation, which disorients and nauseates people who have come in contact with it.

We look at that and the individuals affected and we send something where there are no words, for regaining of the cells of the body of their own integrity in spite of radiation and for the individuals to lose their nausea as their cells regain their own integrity.

We send Love Light to each of the pets that are helping to support the humans that are around them. We see much love is given and we send thanks to each pet for the job that they are doing.

We send Love Light to Team Shift and to each individual working in Team Shift and all the people that will be coming into Team Shift, as it becomes a growing movement that helps individuals adjust to their environment and to become stronger and feel supported and becomes a movement in the world that makes a big difference.

And we thank each and every person on the line, as each of you is so importantly contributing. Each of you is a valued human being and is a part of the movement towards a better more peaceful new world. We leave but we do not leave, as we are always with you, Adonai.
