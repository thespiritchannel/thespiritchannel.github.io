This is the call where people submit questions. Let’s see if there are any good questions that came in—animals on other planets, alright. People hearing high frequency in their ears, those are the latest submissions.

 

In case you happen to be listening to our call for the first time, my name is Wynn Free. In 1999 I had a very close introduction to what I call, “inter-dimensional communication”, beings in other realms that have something to say to us, and something to contribute to us. It started out with David Wilcock and writing the book The Reincarnation of Edgar Cayce? The book has been out for quite some time and is still getting awesome reviews on Amazon:

In any case, this is an introduction to Sources that say they’re group souls in other dimensions that are a helper and a guide for the evolution of this planet and humans.

 

I tell people, “You can’t believe that until you personally experience it.” We have three conference calls a week; we have an archive section. If they are who they say they are, this is kind of an epoch event. Except you have to decide; you have to develop the resonance. It doesn’t help for me or anyone else to say it. We just put out all this stuff for free so you can decide, so you have enough stuff to evaluate it.

 

On our Wednesday call, people submit questions and we ask their questions live on the call. Terry is a—I don’t like to use the word ‘channel, even though that’s what some people would say. Terry is a ‘transmutation station’ in a physical body that is able to hear their words and put them into the English language. We ask them questions and you can listen live.

 

Channeling, this idea of bringing through messages from higher realms, particularly realms this high, is an imprecise science or exercise. There’s possibility for Terry’s own mind to bleed through; there’s even the possibility that a negative source could come in and say something to derail us. I don’t believe that has happened very much in our work, but that’s why it’s so important for you to use your own scrutiny.

 

The purpose of all this is not to make you a follower, even if they are who they say they are; it’s to empower you, for you to have a direct experience of your place in the Universe. You are actually at the center of the Universe where you are. Most of you don’t experience that.

 

Now we’re talking to a Source that says they created the Universe and we have the opportunity to ask them questions. It’s not about the information – I won’t say ‘never’; even though we get a lot of wisdom and great answers, it is not the stimulation of your mental body that is the most important thing, because while you’re on this line, they say they are observing. They say they are connecting with you if you would like that experience. Many people have experienced an expansion of how they feel while they’re listening on this call.

 

It’s very subtle, but it’s not always subtle; it’s an individual thing. Pay attention carefully to the energy around you during the call; pay attention to shifts in your body. Remind yourself of how you were feeling when you came in to the call and how you feel when the call ends. Be a good observer and see if you can find this frequency that they say is them; they use this call as an excuse to make contact with you if you’re open to that.

 

On that note, we have an introduction, an introductory, I don’t know what to call it, calling in of the Light. That helps us bring the energies together and set the mood and the safety for them to come into the space.

 

Father-Mother God we ask for the presence of the Light to surround and protect Terry, myself and everyone listening or reading the transcripts, regardless of location and regardless of time, and that any negativity be taken to the highest realms of Light and transmuted for the highest good of all concerned. We see ourselves in the flow of energy radiating from the center of the Universe, through the galaxies, through the Milky Way, through the solar system, through the outer energy fields of planet earth, through our bodies and anchored into the center of the earth. Right now, we invoke a group energy connection, while maintaining the sovereign integrity of our souls. We invite those Sources that are positive, service-to-others, honoring the Law of One to join with us, as we create a protected space that only the positive has access to and anything not of that nature must leave now.

 

Do we have our Sources present to give an introduction to this session?

 

Ra’An:  We greet you in the Love Light of The One Infinite Creator. This is the 5th of December 2012 and we are connecting with the space of each and every person who wishes to be connected with who is on the call. We do not come through the radio; we do not come through the computer; we do not come through the telephone line; we are non-linearly present in this space. Do you have questions?

 

Wynn:  Yes we do. Our first question is from Ron in Mexico. He has a question about what’s commonly known as the higher self and how to communicate and receive guidance from our higher self.

 

Before I ask Ron’s question, could you explain to us from your point of view what a higher self is?

 

Ra’An:  The higher self of each individual—and each individual has a higher self—each soul has a series of frequencies wherein they are guided; they get guidance. It is the highest and the best of each individual. It can be drawn upon to give guidance. An individual is a composite being and in other religions such as the Hawaii religion of Kahuna, there are references to the lower self. The higher self is of the higher frequencies and provides guidance for the lower self if the lower self can open itself up to be connected with the higher self being, can listen to the wisdom with which the higher self can guide one.

 

Wynn:  When you’re communicating through Terry, are you communicating through her higher self?

 

Ra’An:  We are; it is her higher self and her lower self; her whole self is connected with us. We act like a docking area around the crown of her head. We stabilize ourselves with her at that point. It is all of Terry that is resonating with our energies.

 

Wynn:  What is the connection between the higher self and what’s called the soul?

 

Ra’An:  The soul and the higher self are resonating; the higher self is the being that is the guiding force for the entity and for the soul. The higher self and the soul are synonymous with the description of the individual guidance unit of the being.

 

Wynn:  When an individual is making connection to higher frequencies, oftentimes you describe you yourself as a frequency that we can pick up. Is the higher self the first entry-point for those frequencies and then it gets transmuted in a way that a person can feel them or can speak with your projections?

 

Ra’An:  No; we are resonating with the whole of individual; the higher self, the lower self, we are resonating with the whole. It is the whole that picks us up.

 

Wynn:  So the terminology of higher self and soul are kind of limited in a certain way, as perhaps part of the whole, yes?

 

Ra’An:  It is a guiding part of the whole, if the lower self opens itself up to resonate with the whole.

 

Wynn:  In what density is what’s called the higher self? Would that be different for every person?

 

Ra’An:  It is in the highest within; it is above the astral. It is connected in a point; we are getting out of locations when we describe where it is, as it is not so much in a location as it is the generator of a location.

 

Wynn:  And, does every human being have the potential to connect with their higher self?

 

Ra’An:  Give us a moment. The majority do have the connection capability.

 

Wynn:  Thank you. So now I’m going to ask Ron’s questions here:

 

“Is it useful to visualize having a higher self when meditating and looking inward for guidance?”

 

Ra’An:  We see that this is a question that predisposes that the person is centered in their lower self. If this is the case, the answer is: they can visualize this.

 

If a person is acting as Terry is acting, the higher self is the guiding factor. It is not necessary to visualize this, as she is being her higher self and the lower self is being the higher self.

 

When you speak of visualizing the higher self, this perhaps is the more general condition of most earth beings and the answer is, “Yes, it helps to visualize.” However, when one can get to the state of everything acting in harmony, the lower self and the higher self is all one. It is not needed that one visualize it, because one is being it.

 

Wynn:  Thank you.

 

If one is learning to be their higher self, how would they feel once they accomplish that?

 

Ra’An:  They would lose much of the conflict within themselves and the whole would be working in tandem towards the path of the good of the individual. There would not be duality per se, where one would polarize one direction and then polarize in another direction. There would be a general sensuring [to experience something in it’s entirety rather than it’s component parts] of the individual in the life.

 

Wynn:  Thank you. And, Ron’s second question:

 

“Can you give us insight on how our higher self interacts with angels, guides, Source and our corporal awareness, to guide us to act for the highest good?”

 

Ra’An:  Some of this question was answered in the last answer, as the individual would be working harmoniously within all aspects of their life [wise guidance would be part of it].

 

Wynn:  Thank you.

“Right now there are many people complaining or commenting about hearing high frequency harmonics in their ears, to which I seem to be one of those who have heard those harmonic frequencies constantly in my ears for eight months.”

“So, could you explain what these tones are?”

 

Ra’An:  Thank you.

 

Ringing in the ears can stem from a number of causes. One of them would be the ringing of the earth, as the earth is moving to change it’s orientation in a localized area. These are generally heard within the ears at a duration of a few seconds and the tones are connected with the location on the earth plane, wherein the earth is shifting its harmonics; dolphins can pick up these sounds very easily.

 

Another cause of ringing in the ears is a misalignment within the ears, in the workings of the ear, which can be connected with a misalignment within the kidney area and it is needed to clear the kidneys to help the body; to help the ear clear the area that is misaligned which is creating ringing. This is generally called tinnitus.

 

Give us a moment; there are other causes of ringing and vibrations in the ears which can be hearing the hum of the earth, which is not a misalignment but is an electrical phenomenon that sometimes an individual can pick up and hear. It is of an electrical nature and it is the hum that continues and goes on and on. We would suggest that you observe and see if this is the type of a hum that you are hearing that goes on and on and is the natural humming of the earth. It is a frequency which some people can hear and the majority of people cannot, even though it is going on in everyone’s vicinity.

 

That is our answer.

 

Wynn:  Thank you.

 

This is from Wilbert in Brooklyn, New York. I’ll ask his question as a general question, because I think it might relate to many people. The question is:

 

“Can someone have negative energies around them which come, not from within their own consciousness, but are projected from somewhere else? Can someone have negative energies around them which block them from fully realizing their higher connections?”

Wynn: Yes. Thank you.

Wynn:  There are a lot of other substances besides THC; LSD, alcohol, ecstasy, many of the drugs that people use for some kind of elevated experience. Does the answer work for all of those substances as well?
